Frequently Asked Questions

How much does this cost? ▼

Projects typically start at $5,000 for a complete solution. This includes discovery, design, implementation, and optimization. We'll give you a clear quote after our discovery call — no surprises. If we can't deliver ROI that exceeds the cost, we'll tell you.

How long until I see results? ▼

Most projects go live within 2-4 weeks. You'll start seeing results immediately — whether that's time saved, leads captured, or processes automated. We track metrics from day one so you know exactly what's working.

What if I don't know what I need? ▼

That's exactly what the discovery call is for. You tell us where your business feels stuck — the tasks that take too long, the opportunities you're missing, the friction that's slowing you down. We'll identify where AI can help and what makes sense to tackle first.

Do I need to be technical? ▼

Not at all. We handle all the technical work. You focus on your business. We'll explain everything in plain English and make sure you're comfortable with how it all works.

What industries do you work with? ▼

Any business with friction we can fix. Service businesses, professional services, agencies, e-commerce, local businesses — if you have processes that could be smarter, leads that need handling, or tasks that need automating, we can help.

Do you require long-term contracts? ▼

No. Each project is priced individually. If you want ongoing support or optimization, that's available month-to-month. If we're not delivering value, you shouldn't be paying us.
