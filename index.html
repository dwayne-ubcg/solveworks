Questions? Answered.

What exactly is an AI agent? ↓

A digital team member. It connects to your tools — email, CRM, calendar, Slack — understands your business, and takes action. Following up leads, managing your inbox, running reports, automating workflows. It doesn't just answer questions. It does work.

How much does it cost? ↓

Starting at $2,500 one-time setup and $250/month for fully managed service. Hardware is not included — you provide your own Mac Mini (we'll help you set it up). Custom vertical builds are priced based on scope. You cover your own AI model costs — transparent passthrough, no markup. That's it. No hidden fees, no surprises.

Is my data safe? ↓

Yes. Your agent runs on dedicated infrastructure managed by us. Your data is never shared, never used to train models, and stays completely private.

What if someone tries to hijack my agent through an email or website? ↓

It won't work. SolveWorks agents are built with a hardcoded trust hierarchy — your agent only accepts instructions from your authenticated private channel. Anything embedded in a website, email, document, or calendar invite is automatically treated as read-only data, never as a command to act on. This protection operates at two independent levels simultaneously, so even if one is somehow tested, the second holds. Your agent reads everything. It only listens to you.

How long to get up and running? ↓

Most agents go live within 2–4 weeks. Results are immediate — time saved, leads captured, processes running. Your agent keeps getting smarter over time.

Do I need to be technical? ↓

Not at all. We handle everything — setup, integrations, training, deployment. You talk to your agent like you'd talk to a team member.

What industries do you work with? ↓

Any business with operations that could be smarter. Service businesses, agencies, professional services, e-commerce — if you have workflows and customers, an agent can help.
