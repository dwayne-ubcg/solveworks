Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

7 Cellars Ltd. ("we", "us") operates the 7 Cellars QuickBooks integration application. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Access

When you connect your QuickBooks Online account, we access: company information, chart of accounts, invoices, bills, journal entries, and related financial data necessary for business operations.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your QuickBooks data solely to: (a) synchronize accounting records with 7 Cellars internal systems; (b) generate financial reports; (c) manage chart of accounts and invoicing workflows.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your QuickBooks data with any third parties. Data is accessed only by authorized 7 Cellars personnel.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We retain data only as long as necessary for business operations and comply with applicable data protection regulations.

5. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time by disconnecting the application from QuickBooks and contacting us at dwayne@7cellars.ky.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
