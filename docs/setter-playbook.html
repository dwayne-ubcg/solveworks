SolveWorks Setter — Product Playbook

What It Is

An AI-powered lead follow-up system that responds to every inbound lead in under 60 seconds — via SMS, iMessage, and email — qualifies them through natural conversation, and books calls directly into the client’s calendar. Runs 24/7 on dedicated hardware the client owns.

Think of it as a tireless sales setter that never misses a lead, never forgets to follow up, and costs a fraction of a human hire.

What the Client Gets

Instant Lead Response

  • Every inbound lead gets a personalized response within 60 seconds
  • SMS and iMessage (blue bubbles — we’re the only ones who can do this)
  • Works nights, weekends, holidays — never off

AI-Powered Qualification

  • Natural back-and-forth conversation (not robotic templates)
  • Asks qualifying questions based on the client’s criteria
  • Identifies hot leads vs tire kickers
  • Learns the client’s offer, tone, and objection handling

Automated Booking

  • Qualified leads get pushed to book a call via Calendly/calendar link
  • Confirmation messages sent automatically
  • Reminder nudges before the call (24hr + 1hr)
  • No-show follow-up sequences

Persistent Follow-Up

  • Leads who go cold get follow-up sequences (day 1, 3, 7, 14, 30)
  • Different messaging at each stage — not just “hey, still interested?”
  • Stops automatically when the lead books, buys, or opts out

Dashboard

  • Real-time view of all conversations
  • Lead status: New → Contacted → Qualifying → Booked → No-Show → Won/Lost
  • Booking rate, response time, and conversation metrics
  • Accessible from any device at solveworks.io/[client]/setter

How It Works (Client Setup)

  1. Client buys Mac Mini — we ship pre-configured or set up on-site
  2. 30-minute onboarding call — we learn their offer, ideal customer, qualifying questions, tone
  3. We configure everything — Twilio number, AI scripts, calendar integration, dashboard
  4. Test with real leads — run for 48 hours with client reviewing conversations
  5. Go live — leads start flowing, client watches the dashboard fill up

Time from signup to live: 3-5 business days

Metrics We Track

  • Speed to lead: Average first response time (target: <60 seconds)
  • Response rate: % of leads who reply to first message
  • Qualification rate: % of leads who pass qualifying questions
  • Booking rate: % of qualified leads who book a call
  • Show rate: % of booked calls where the lead actually shows up
  • Cost per booking: Total monthly cost ÷ bookings
