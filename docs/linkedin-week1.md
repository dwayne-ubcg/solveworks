## Wednesday — Insight (Educational)

Your AI assistant should run on YOUR hardware. Here's why:

Every time you use a cloud-based AI tool, your emails, contacts, business data, and client info pass through someone else's servers.

For most small businesses, that's a risk you don't need to take.

We set up each client with a dedicated Mac Mini running their own AI agent. It handles:

→ Email triage and drafting
→ Calendar and scheduling
→ Lead generation and outreach
→ CRM updates
→ Weekly reporting

All from a single dashboard we call Mission Control.

The data never leaves the machine. No third-party access. No "we updated our privacy policy" surprises.

Privacy isn't a feature. It's the architecture.

If you're a business owner exploring AI — ask where your data lives. The answer matters.
