## 🔑 Key Pain Points to Explore

### Pain 1: No EA — Multi-Business Chaos
He has no executive assistant managing his day across three businesses. This is the Brit/Mika use case.
- **Ask:** "Walk me through a typical morning. How do you decide what gets your attention first?"
- **Ask:** "How do you keep track of what's happening across BenDial, the supplements business, and Aspire?"
- **Ask:** "What falls through the cracks most often?"

### Pain 2: AI SDR Pipeline Visibility
He's already running AI SDRs for Aspire Payments. But does he have visibility into the full funnel?
- **Ask:** "Your AI SDRs are booking calls — where do those leads live? Can you see your whole pipeline at a glance?"
- **Ask:** "When a hot lead transfers to you, how much context do you have on what the AI already discussed?"
- **Listen for:** manual tracking, scattered spreadsheets, no unified view

### Pain 3: Call Centre Management (BenDial)
Running a call centre in Belize remotely from Boca Raton = visibility problem.
- **Ask:** "How do you track agent performance at BenDial? Close rates, call volume, quality?"
- **Ask:** "Are you listening to calls or reviewing transcripts?"
- **Listen for:** flying blind, relying on managers to report up

### Pain 4: Media Spend / Nutraceuticals ROI
Ad spend on supplements can be massive. Is he tracking ROI properly?
- **Ask:** "What's your monthly ad spend on the supplements side? Do you know your cost per acquisition?"
- **Ask:** "How do you know which channels are working?"
- **Listen for:** high spend, unclear attribution, gut-feel decisions
