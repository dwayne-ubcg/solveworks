How a 3-location dental practice eliminated missed calls, cut no-shows by 40%, and recovered an estimated $180K in annual revenue.

The Practice

Coastal Dental Group is a family-owned dental practice operating three locations with 12 staff, including 4 dentists. They see roughly 150 patients per week across all locations and had been growing steadily — but their operations weren't keeping up.

The Problem

The practice had three major friction points bleeding time and revenue:

✕

Missed calls during procedures

Dentists and hygienists are hands-deep in procedures 80% of the day. The front desk handles what they can, but during busy periods, 30-40% of calls went to voicemail. Most callers don't leave messages — they call the next practice on Google.

✕

No-shows eating revenue

A 15% no-show rate across all locations. Each empty chair costs an average of $350 in lost revenue. Manual reminder calls were inconsistent — staff simply didn't have time to call every patient.

✕

Receptionist overwhelm

The front desk was spending 3+ hours per day on appointment confirmations, rescheduling, and answering routine questions (hours, insurance, directions). Zero time left for patient experience.

The practice owner knew they were losing patients and revenue but felt stuck — hiring another receptionist per location seemed like the only option, at $40K+ per position annually.

The Solution

SolveWorks implemented a three-part AI system over 3 weeks:

1. AI Voice & Chat Assistant

A conversational AI that handles incoming calls and website chats 24/7. It answers common questions (hours, insurance accepted, services offered), qualifies new patient inquiries, and books appointments directly into the practice management system. When it can't handle something, it routes to staff with full context.

2. Smart Appointment Management

Automated confirmation sequences via text and email — sent at 72 hours, 24 hours, and 2 hours before each appointment. Patients can confirm, reschedule, or cancel with a single tap. Cancellations automatically trigger waitlist outreach to fill the slot.

3. Patient Follow-Up Engine

Automated post-visit follow-ups: care instructions after procedures, recall reminders for cleanings, and personalized Google Review requests timed perfectly after positive visits.

The Results

✓

No-show rate dropped from 15% to 9%

Smart reminders with easy rescheduling meant patients either showed up or rebooked. Waitlist fills recovered 60% of cancelled slots.

✓

Zero missed calls during business hours

AI handles the overflow. After hours, it books appointments and captures leads that previously disappeared.

✓

Receptionists freed up 3 hours per day

Time redirected to patient experience — greeting patients, insurance verification, treatment plan coordination.

✓

Google Reviews increased 3x

Automated review requests sent at the right moment. Went from 2-3 reviews/month to 8-10.

✓

~$180K in annual revenue recovered

From reduced no-shows, captured after-hours leads, and improved patient retention. ROI within 6 weeks of launch.

What the Owner Said

"We were about to hire two more receptionists. Instead, the AI handles the volume and our existing team focuses on the patients in front of them. The math was a no-brainer."
