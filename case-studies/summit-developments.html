How a boutique real estate developer captured 3x more qualified leads and cut administrative overhead by 70% — with a team of just 4 people.

The Company

Summit Developments is a boutique real estate development firm specializing in high-end residential builds. A lean team of 4 — the owner, a project manager, an admin coordinator, and a sales associate — handling everything from land acquisition to client handover on projects ranging from $800K to $3M.

The Problem

Small team, big ambitions, too much friction. Three issues were holding them back:

✕

Leads dying on the vine

Inquiries came through the website, Zillow, email, and phone. The sales associate triaged them manually — but with 150+ monthly inquiries and only one person, response times averaged 8-12 hours. By then, serious buyers had moved on. Worse, 40% of leads never got a response at all.

✕

Document chaos

Every project involves dozens of documents — permits, contracts, change orders, invoices from subcontractors, inspection reports. The admin coordinator spent 15+ hours per week filing, extracting data, and manually updating tracking spreadsheets. Errors were common, and finding a specific document often took 20+ minutes.

✕

No follow-up system

Interested prospects who didn't convert immediately were "put on a list" that nobody ever worked. Past clients who might refer or buy again got zero proactive outreach. Revenue was left on the table every month.

The owner estimated they were losing 3-5 serious buyers per month to slow follow-up alone — at an average commission of $40K per project, that's a devastating number for a small operation.

The Solution

SolveWorks built a two-phase system rolled out over 4 weeks:

Phase 1: AI Lead Qualification & Nurture

An AI-powered lead management system that:

  • Responds to every inquiry within 2 minutes — via chat, email auto-reply, or text
  • Asks qualifying questions (budget, timeline, location preferences, build type)
  • Scores leads automatically: hot leads get instant alerts to the sales associate; warm leads enter a nurture sequence; unqualified leads get a polite redirect
  • Runs personalized follow-up sequences for warm leads — weekly touches that sound human, not robotic
  • Re-engages past clients quarterly with market updates and referral prompts

Phase 2: Document Intelligence

An AI document processing system that:

  • Automatically categorizes incoming documents (invoices, permits, contracts, etc.)
  • Extracts key data — amounts, dates, contractor names, permit numbers — and populates tracking sheets
  • Creates a searchable document library: find any document in seconds with natural language search
  • Flags anomalies: duplicate invoices, expired permits, unsigned contracts

The Results

✓

100% of leads now get a response within 2 minutes

Up from 60% within 12 hours. The sales associate now only talks to pre-qualified, interested buyers.

✓

Qualified lead pipeline tripled

Not from more traffic — from actually capturing and qualifying the leads that were already coming in.

✓

Admin time cut from 15+ hours/week to ~4 hours

Document processing that took a full day now takes minutes. The admin coordinator now focuses on project coordination instead of data entry.

✓

2 deals closed from re-engaged past clients in first quarter

Automated nurture sequences brought back clients who had gone quiet — $240K in project value from zero additional marketing spend.

✓

Zero document-related errors in first 3 months

AI extraction eliminated manual data entry errors. Duplicate invoice detection caught $12K in overpayments in the first month alone.

What the Owner Said

"We were drowning in admin and missing opportunities we didn't even know about. Now our 4-person team operates like a company twice our size. The document processing alone saved us from hiring someone."
