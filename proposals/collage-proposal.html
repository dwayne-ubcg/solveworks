Questions & Answers

Common Questions

Straight answers — no pitch

🚀 "We tried outbound before and it didn't work — why is this different?"

The sequences you ran were generic cadences — same message to everyone. This system researches each prospect individually before drafting. It knows what they do, how big they are, what HR system they're likely on, and whether they'd care about Canadian payroll compliance. That research becomes the email. Personalization at scale beats generic automation every time.

🔌 "Does it integrate with Pipedrive, Gmail, and Avoma?"

Yes. Pipedrive is the deal record — the system creates and updates deals automatically. Gmail is where follow-up drafts land and where email triage runs. Avoma is the call recording source — the agent pulls recordings post-call and analyzes them. Calendly triggers the pre-call research brief. Everything plugs into the tools you're already using.

✍️ "Do the AEs lose control over their own outreach?"

No. Nothing sends without approval. The outbound queue is a draft list that you or the AE reviews before anything goes out. Post-call follow-ups land in Gmail drafts — the rep reads it, edits if needed, and hits send. The agent removes the "writing from scratch" problem, not the human judgment. Your team stays in control of their relationships.

⏱️ "How long until we're live?"

Three to four weeks from kickoff to full deployment. Week one: tool integrations (Pipedrive, Gmail, Avoma, Calendly) and ICP definition. Weeks two to three: build and test with real calls and real prospect lists. Week four: go live with outbound engine running, coaching live, and dashboard active. We're on standby during go-live week.

🔒 "Where does our data live?"

The system runs on a dedicated Mac Mini in your environment. Your call recordings, prospect data, and pipeline details stay in your infrastructure — not on shared cloud servers. The AI models process data in transit (via your own Anthropic API key, which you control), but persistent storage stays with you.

🚪 "What if we cancel?"

No long-term contracts. Month-to-month. If you cancel, the automations stop and you keep the Mac Mini hardware. All data — prospect lists, call analyses, pipeline records — is always yours. We'd rather earn your business every month than lock you in.
