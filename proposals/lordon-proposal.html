Questions & Answers

Common Questions

Straight answers — no pitch

🔗 "How does it connect to Lightspeed, Shopify, and Omnisend?"

Through official APIs — the same secure connections other business tools use. We set up the integrations during onboarding. Your logins stay yours. The agent reads and writes data through authorized channels, never by screen-scraping or workarounds.

📍 "Does it really handle all three locations?"

Yes. Each Lightspeed location connects individually. When you ask "How's Halifax doing?" it pulls Halifax data. When you ask "Total sales across all stores" it aggregates. You can compare locations, transfer between them, and get consolidated or location-specific reports — all in the same conversation.

🛍️ "Can it actually change things on my Shopify store?"

Yes — product updates, collection changes, discount codes, page edits. SolveWorks built and manages an entire Shopify store (7cellars.ky) through chat. Same approach, tailored for your boutique. You stay in control: the agent proposes changes, you approve, it executes.

⏱️ "How long until we're up and running?"

Three to four weeks from kickoff. Week one connects your systems and loads your product catalog. Weeks two and three we build out the intelligence layer — brand tracking, morning briefings, customer segmentation. Week four is go-live with hands-on support.

📱 "Do I need to learn new software?"

No. You text your agent via Telegram — same as texting anyone. Send a question, get an answer. Give an instruction, it gets done. The dashboard is a single web page you can check from your phone. If you can text, you can use this.

🚪 "What if we cancel?"

No long-term contracts. Month-to-month. If you cancel, automations stop and you keep the Mac Mini hardware. Your data is always yours — nothing is locked into our system. We'd rather earn your business every month than lock you in.
