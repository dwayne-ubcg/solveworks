What We'll Build

Five targeted capabilities that fill the gaps your current tools don't cover
🎯

Prospect Auto-Vetting & Briefing Cards

Every call starts with context. Zero manual research.

Before a rep picks up the phone, a briefing card is already waiting. The agent pulls data from LinkedIn, company websites, bios, and about pages to build a one-page summary: what the company does, their mission and focus area, the prospect's role, their skill sets, and industry niche. No more spending 10 minutes per prospect clicking through tabs. The rep opens the card, scans it in 30 seconds, and dials with confidence. This is the single biggest time-saver per call.

LinkedIn data pulled Company website scraped Role & skills identified Industry niche tagged Ready before the call
🔥

Email Priority Flagging & Triage

Hot emails surfaced immediately. Nothing slips through.

The agent monitors rep inboxes and flags emails by priority. A prospect who says "ready to move forward" gets flagged immediately. If that email sits unhandled for more than a set window, an alert fires to Joshua or the rep. No more spot-checking inboxes manually and hoping you catch the important ones. Priority emails get handled first because they're visually separated from everything else. The agent learns what "hot" looks like for your specific operation over time.

Rep Activity Dashboard

Every rep's performance at a glance. No manual tracking.

A single dashboard showing calls made, emails handled vs. unhandled, average response times, and task completion for every rep. Filter by day, week, or custom range. See who's on top of their inbox and who needs attention. Replace the manual spot-check with a live view that updates itself. This isn't about micromanaging. It's about knowing where to help before small problems become missed deals.
