Straight answers — no pitch

🤖 "We've played with ChatGPT — how is this different?"

ChatGPT is a sandbox — you ask it things, it answers. Your AI agent is a worker. It's connected to your email, your Klaviyo, your CRM, your customer database. It proactively reaches out to customers, monitors tender sites, manages your content calendar, and runs your margin calculations. You don't have to ask it to do things — it does them and reports back.

📱 "Do we need to learn new software?"

No. You text your agent via Telegram — same as texting anyone. Send a question, get an answer. Ask for a margin calculation, get a breakdown. The dashboard is a single web page you can check from your phone. No training courses. No login credentials to forget.

🔧 "Can it really learn our welding supply customers' patterns?"

Yes. We load your customer order history during onboarding. The agent identifies patterns — who orders what, how often, typical quantities. After a few cycles it's accurately predicting reorder needs. You review and approve every outreach before it goes. Think of it as a really smart assistant who memorized every customer's habits.

💰 "What's this Anthropic subscription?"

Anthropic makes the AI brain (Claude) that powers your agent. Instead of us marking it up, you manage your own subscription directly — about $100 USD/month. Full transparency. You see exactly what you're paying for the AI, and we set it up for you during onboarding. No hidden fees, no middleman markup.

⏱️ "How long until we're up and running?"

Three to four weeks from kickoff. Week one is discovery — we learn your business, your brand, your customers. Weeks two and three we build and configure. Week four we go live with close monitoring. You'll have a working AI employee within a month.

🚪 "What if we cancel?"

No long-term contracts. Month-to-month. If you cancel, automations stop and you keep the Mac Mini hardware. Your data is always yours. We'd rather earn your business every month than lock you into something you can't exit.
