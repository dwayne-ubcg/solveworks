Aspire Payments processes high-risk merchant accounts. That process alone eats hours per merchant — and it's just the beginning. There's daily volume reconciliation across partner dashboards, call center accounting that burns 4-6 hours every week, and client follow-ups that fall through the cracks because there's no system catching them.

The tools are there — Monday.com, Fillout, Persona, DocuSign, QuickBooks — but nothing connects them. Every handoff between systems is a manual copy-paste. Every step is another chance for something to slip. And there's nobody else to catch it, because Jimmy is the entire operation.

When you're running three businesses with no assistant, the question isn't whether you're working hard enough. It's whether your time is being spent on the work that actually grows the business — or on the repetitive processes that should run themselves.
