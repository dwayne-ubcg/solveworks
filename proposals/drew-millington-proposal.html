🎙️

Sales Call Coaching Engine

Charlie AI + NMBR call analysis

You upload or forward a call recording. The agent transcribes it, breaks it into phases (opening, pain discovery, budget, commitment), and scores each phase against Sandler methodology — did you get to pain? Did you establish an up-front contract? Were you the one asking the questions?

It also applies persuasion psychology principles — framing, reciprocity, social proof usage, commitment/consistency — and flags missed opportunities. You get a concise scorecard with 3-5 specific actions for your next call. Over time, it tracks your improvement trends.

Sandler scoring Persuasion analysis Actionable feedback Trend tracking Works for both businesses
🔍

NMBR Lead Intelligence System

Automated prospecting for Canadian payroll opportunities

The agent continuously monitors LinkedIn posts, job boards (Indeed, LinkedIn Jobs, Glassdoor), and industry signals for companies showing intent to hire in Canada or expand payroll operations north. Key triggers: "Canadian payroll" job postings, "expanding to Canada" announcements, payroll engineer roles, cross-border hiring discussions.

It also mines intelligence from your own NMBR calls — mentions of competitors, pain points, expansion plans — and cross-references them with web signals. Leads are delivered with context: company size, signal source, suggested angle of approach.

LinkedIn monitoring Job board scanning Call intel mining Lead scoring Approach suggestions
