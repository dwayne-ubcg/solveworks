What We Heard

From our conversation with Joe about HitLights

HitLights is a $20M eCommerce business selling LED strip lights and low-voltage power supplies across Amazon and Shopify. With 20–25 employees and a customer service team based in the Philippines, you're handling roughly 1,000 customer contacts per month — tech support questions, order status inquiries, and sales messages all flowing through email, voicemail, and phone.

Your strategic priority is clear: grow the professional installer business. Electricians and electrical contractors represent higher average order values and repeat purchases. You want to get in front of new pros, convert them, and keep them coming back. But right now, your team is spread across Re:amaze, Aircall, Go High Level, and Google Suite — with no structured AI implementation despite individual team members experimenting on their own.

You told us customer service is the biggest opportunity — routing inquiries to the right person, automating common responses, and covering after-hours contacts. You also acknowledged that deals have slipped through the cracks before. And every Monday morning, you're manually checking emails, pulling weekend sales reports, verifying inventory levels, and reviewing MTD numbers across multiple tools.
