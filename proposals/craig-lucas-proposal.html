What We'll Build

One AI agent that handles both sides of your operation — countertops and flooring

Countertop Estimating & Remnant Analysis

Slab layouts, seam placement, and usable remnant calculation — automated.

Send your agent the 2D drawings for a countertop job. It analyzes the layout and calculates: how many slabs are needed, where seams should go for optimal yield, total waste percentage, and — the part that matters most — which remnant pieces are large enough to resell or reuse. Pieces 22" wide or greater, 26" or greater, 36" or greater — all identified and valued automatically. Output includes price per square foot, waste amount, usable remnant credit, and total job price. Ready to populate straight into your CRM.

Slab layout optimization Seam placement logic Usable remnant identification Price per sq ft output CRM-ready data
📧

Inbound Email Quote Automation

Jobs arrive by email. Pricing starts before anyone opens them.

When a quote request lands in your inbox, the agent reads the email, extracts the job details — dimensions, material preferences, special requirements — and auto-generates preliminary pricing. By the time Shelley opens the email, the first pass is already done. She reviews, adjusts if needed, and sends the quote. What used to start from scratch now starts 80% complete.

Auto-reads inbound emails Extracts job details Generates preliminary pricing Shelley reviews & approves

Revenue Leakage Detection

Work that happens should always become work that gets invoiced.

The agent cross-references completed work against invoiced work. Jobs that were scheduled but never billed, change orders that were approved but never invoiced, extra materials that were delivered but never charged — it catches the gaps before they become lost revenue. A daily or weekly leakage report shows exactly what's fallen through the cracks so nothing walks out the door unbilled.
