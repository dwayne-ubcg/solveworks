Your First Day With
Your AI Agent

Here's what to expect, what to have ready, and how to get the most out of your agent from day one.

What Happens First

Your agent will reach out on Telegram and walk you through three conversations. Think of it like onboarding a new executive assistant — except this one has a perfect memory and works 24/7.

1

The Brain Dump

Your agent will ask about you, your business, your goals, and how you work. No prep needed — just talk. It'll ask the right questions and organize everything behind the scenes.

Topics covered: What your business does • Your team • Your typical day • What you hate doing manually • Your 90-day goals • How you like to communicate
2

Dashboard Setup

Your agent comes with a personal Mission Control dashboard — a single screen for everything that matters. Your agent will walk you through what can be connected and set up the ones you want.

Available connections: Google Calendar • Email • CRM • Accounting • Social Media • Project Management • Custom Data
3

Brand Standards

If your agent is going to create content, write emails, or produce anything outward-facing, it needs to know your brand. Share what you have — or your agent will build your brand standards from scratch through conversation.

What it builds: Brand voice & tone • Color palette • Logo guidelines • Audience profile • Content pillars • Do's and don'ts

What to Expect

💬

Telegram is your direct line

Text your agent like you'd text a colleague. Ask questions, give instructions, share ideas. It's always listening and always ready.

📊

Your dashboard updates in real time

We'll give you a private URL and password. Bookmark it — everything you've connected shows up here, always current.

🧠

It learns fast

The more you interact in the first few days, the smarter it gets. Don't hold back — tell it what you like, what you don't, and what matters.

🔒

You're always in control

Your agent won't send emails, post on social media, or take any external action without your explicit approval.

Tips for Day One

1

Just talk naturally

No special commands or syntax. Text your agent like a person.

2

Brain dump everything

The more context you give early, the better everything works from day one.

3

Have your logins handy

Dashboard setup goes faster when you can connect things in real time.

4

Tell it what annoys you

Hate long messages? Want bullet points? Prefer morning updates only? Say so. It'll adapt.

5

Ask it anything

Even if you're not sure it can help — ask. You'll be surprised.
