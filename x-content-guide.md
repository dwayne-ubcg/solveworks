## Writing Rules

### Length
- **Regular posts:** Under 200 chars. Punchy beats long.
- **Threads:** Each tweet punchy, but threads can go deep (5-10 tweets)

### Hashtags
- Max 2, only when truly relevant
- Good: `#AIagents` `#solopreneur` `#buildinpublic`
- Never: generic spam like `#AI #tech #business #entrepreneur`

### Formatting
- Line breaks matter. One idea per line.
- Emojis: 1-2 max. Use when it adds tone, not decoration.
- Threads: Start with a 🧵 hook, end with CTA

### Ending Every Post
**Always end with one of:**
- A question ("How are you handling this?")
- A CTA ("Reply if you want to see how")
- A surprising reversal ("The results shocked us too")
- An invitation ("Thread below 👇")

## What to NEVER Post

❌ "AI is changing everything" — too vague  
❌ "We're excited to..." — corporate cringe  
❌ Threaded posts with 15 tweets of fluff  
❌ Posts without a concrete example or insight  
❌ Anything that sounds like it was written by ChatGPT with no editing  
❌ Engagement bait without substance ("like this if you agree!")  
❌ More than 2 hashtags
