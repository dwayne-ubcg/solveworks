Pro Tips
03 — PRO TIPS

Better Prompts,
Better Results.

The #1 thing that makes your agent more powerful: specificity. Here's what that looks like in practice.

✗ WEAK "Write me an email"
✓ STRONG "Write a follow-up email to John at Acme Corp about the pricing we discussed Tuesday. Friendly tone, mention the 15% volume discount."
✗ WEAK "Find me leads"
✓ STRONG "Find 20 HR directors at manufacturing companies in Ontario with 100–500 employees. Include email and LinkedIn."
✗ WEAK "Make a report"
✓ STRONG "Pull last month's sales data and create a report showing top 10 clients by revenue, with month-over-month comparison."
1

Be specific — context makes everything better. The more you give, the better the output.

2

Give background — "We just had a meeting with X and they mentioned Y." Your agent uses this context to get it right the first time.

3

Ask it to confirm before sending — tell it to show you the output first. "Draft this, but don't send it." Good habit, especially early on.

4

Correct it when it's off — say "that's not quite right, here's what I meant." It adjusts immediately and remembers your preferences going forward.

5

Use voice when typing is slow — hold the microphone button in Telegram, talk naturally. It transcribes and responds just like text.

Setting Expectations
04 — WHAT TO EXPECT

Honest Answers,
No Surprises.

Your agent is remarkably capable. Here's what to expect as you get going — and a few situations where a human touch still makes sense.

⚡

Works great — a few things to know

  • Complex responses take 30–60 seconds — it's thinking carefully, not frozen. Wait for it.
  • First-time tasks take a little longer while it learns your patterns and preferences.
  • It may ask clarifying questions before acting — that's intentional. A good question now beats a wrong answer later.
  • Always review before it sends anything to clients on your behalf — you're the final check.
⚠️

Best to avoid these scenarios

  • Visual website builders like Wix or Squarespace — their drag-and-drop editors are iframe-based and don't play well with AI automation.
  • Directly editing live financial or accounting systems without reviewing the changes first.
  • Tasks requiring real-time phone conversations — your agent is text-based (and excellent at it).
  • Decisions that require your judgment — your agent will research, analyze, and recommend. You make the call.
💡

The most successful clients treat their agent like a brilliant new hire — one that needs good instructions at first, but gets faster and smarter about your business every single week.

Security & Privacy
06 — SECURITY & PRIVACY

Your Data Stays
Yours. Always.

We built this system with privacy as a first principle — not as an afterthought. Here's exactly how it works.

🖥️

Runs on your hardware

Your agent lives on a dedicated Mac Mini — in your office or home. It's not running on a shared server somewhere. It's yours.

🔒

Data stays local

Your business data never leaves your machine. We don't collect it, store it in the cloud, or have access to it unless you explicitly share it for support.

📲

Telegram authentication

Only messages from your authenticated Telegram account are treated as instructions. Someone else typing to your agent does nothing.

🛡️

Injection-resistant

Your agent is trained to recognize and reject prompt injection attempts — when external content tries to hijack it with fake instructions. It flags these and ignores them.

🔐

The bottom line: We don't see your data unless you explicitly share it with us for support. Your agent's intelligence is powered by AI models, but your business information lives on your machine, behind your door.
