What OpenClaw Does For Property Managers

The repetitive, time-consuming work that keeps you stuck in reactive mode — handled automatically.

💬

Tenant Communication

Tenant texts at 11pm about a lease question? AI answers instantly with accurate info from their lease. Maintenance request? Logged, categorized, and acknowledged before you even see it.

🔧

Maintenance Triage & Vendor Dispatch

AI categorizes requests by urgency — burst pipe gets escalated immediately, squeaky door goes to the queue. Automatically contacts the right vendor based on issue type and property location.

📋

Lease Renewal Tracking

90-day, 60-day, 30-day reminders sent automatically. AI drafts renewal offers based on market rates, tracks responses, and flags tenants who haven't replied so you can follow up personally.

🤝

Vendor Coordination & Invoicing

AI sends work orders to vendors, follows up on completion, collects invoices, and matches them to the right property and owner account. No more chasing contractors for paperwork.

🔍

Tenant Screening Assistance

AI collects application info, runs preliminary checks against your criteria, organizes documents, and flags incomplete applications — before you spend time reviewing them.

📊

Financial Reporting & Owner Updates

Monthly owner reports generated automatically — rent collected, expenses, maintenance completed, occupancy rates. Owners get professional updates without you spending hours in spreadsheets.
