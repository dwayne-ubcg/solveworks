What Your Agent Does For Accounting Firms

The admin work that eats your margins and burns out your team — handled before anyone notices.

📁

Client Document Collection

AI sends personalized document requests to each client, follows up automatically (politely, persistently), and tracks what's been received vs. what's still missing. No more "did you get my T4?"

📅

Tax Deadline Tracking

Every client's filing deadline tracked automatically. Your team gets alerts weeks in advance. Clients get reminders to submit documents. Nothing falls through the cracks — even during tax season chaos.

👋

Client Intake & Onboarding

New client signs up — AI sends the engagement letter, collects entity info, requests access to prior year returns, and sets up their file. First impression: professional and seamless.

💰

Billing & Invoice Follow-Ups

Invoices sent on time. Overdue reminders sent automatically — friendly at first, firmer over time. No more awkward "just checking in on that invoice" emails from you personally.

📧

Email Triage & Prioritization

AI reads incoming emails, categorizes by urgency and client, drafts responses for routine questions, and flags anything that needs your personal attention. Your inbox becomes manageable again.

📝

Meeting Prep & Summaries

Before every client meeting, AI pulls together their file status, outstanding items, recent communications, and key discussion points. After the meeting, it generates a summary with action items.
