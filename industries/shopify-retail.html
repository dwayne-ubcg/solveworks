What OpenClaw Does For Shopify Retailers

The operational grind that keeps you stuck working IN the business instead of ON it — automated.

📦

Inventory Alerts & Reorder Notifications

AI monitors stock levels across all SKUs. When your best-seller hits reorder point, you get an alert with supplier info and suggested PO quantity. No more surprise stockouts killing your revenue.

💬

Customer Support Automation

"Where's my order?" "Do you have this in medium?" "What's your return policy?" AI handles 80% of support tickets instantly with accurate, on-brand responses. Complex issues get routed to you with full context.

🔄

Order Issue Resolution

Wrong item shipped? Damaged in transit? AI initiates the return/exchange process, sends the customer a prepaid label, and triggers the replacement — before it becomes a 1-star review.

🏭

Supplier Communication & PO Management

AI drafts purchase orders, sends them to suppliers, tracks confirmations, follows up on late shipments, and alerts you to lead time changes. Your supply chain runs smoother without constant babysitting.

⭐

Review Monitoring & Response

New review posted? AI alerts you immediately. Positive reviews get a thank-you response. Negative reviews get flagged with a drafted response for your approval — fast enough to turn a complaint into a save.

📈

Sales Reporting & Trend Alerts

Daily sales summaries. Weekly trend analysis. AI spots patterns you'd miss — a product suddenly trending, conversion rate dropping on a collection page, or a promo code getting shared publicly.
