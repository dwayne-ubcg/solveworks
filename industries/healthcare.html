What Your Agent Does For Your Practice

Every task burying your front desk — done automatically, around the clock, without adding headcount.

📅

Appointment Scheduling & Reminders

Patients book online or by text. AI sends confirmation, reminder 48h before, and day-of nudge. No-shows drop dramatically when every patient gets a personal touchpoint.

🔄

Recall System Automation

6-month cleanings, annual eye exams, chiropractic follow-ups — AI tracks every patient's recall schedule and reaches out automatically. No more patients slipping through the cracks.

📋

New Patient Intake & Onboarding

Before they walk in the door, new patients get welcome messages, digital intake forms, insurance info requests, and what-to-expect guides. First impressions on autopilot.

🛡️

Insurance Verification & Pre-Auth

AI follows up on insurance verifications and pre-authorization requests so your staff isn't on hold for hours. Patients arrive with coverage confirmed and no billing surprises.

⭐

Review Generation

Automated post-visit review requests sent at the perfect moment — when the patient is happy and the experience is fresh. Watch your Google rating climb without asking anyone to "leave a review."

💬

Patient Communication

Post-treatment instructions, follow-up care reminders, and check-in messages. Patients feel cared for between visits, and your staff doesn't have to make 50 calls a day.

👥

Staff Scheduling & Coordination

Shift reminders, coverage requests, schedule changes — AI handles the coordination so your office manager isn't texting 12 people to fill a Tuesday afternoon.

🤝

Referral Tracking & Thank-Yous

When a patient refers someone, AI tracks it, sends a thank-you message, and keeps the referral loop warm. Your best marketing channel — finally systemized.

💳

Billing & Payment Follow-Ups

Outstanding balances get friendly, automated reminders — no awkward phone calls from your staff. Payment links included. Collections rate goes up, friction goes down.

🌙

After-Hours Patient Inquiries

Patients don't only need you 9-to-5. AI answers questions, triages urgency, and books appointments at midnight — so you're not losing new patients to the practice that picks up the phone.
