### Key Trend: AI Is Causing Layoffs AND Creating Staffing Demand
- Block, eBay, and others explicitly citing AI as reason for cuts
- BUT: companies implementing AI need humans to manage the transition
- Roles emerging: AI implementation managers, AI trainers, data quality specialists, AI-augmented marketing, AI-enabled financial analysts
- **Rylem opportunity:** Position as a firm that places "AI-ready" professionals — people who can work alongside AI tools, not be replaced by them

### Key Trend: Contract-First Hiring Is the New Default
- Companies that laid off permanent employees are rebuilding with contractors first
- Lower risk, faster scaling, easier to adjust headcount
- This is the fundamental tailwind for Rylem's business model
- Every major layoff = future contract staffing demand 60-90 days later

### Key Trend: Staffing Industry Consolidation
- Large firms acquiring smaller players
- Technology differentiation (AI-powered sourcing, CRM automation) separating winners from losers
- Rylem's investment in AI assistant + Ceipal automation is a competitive advantage vs. other mid-market firms
