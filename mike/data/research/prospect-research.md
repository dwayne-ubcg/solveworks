## Key Themes for Nationwide Outreach

1. **Post-layoff contract wave:** Tech companies nationwide (not just Seattle) cutting perm headcount and backfilling with contractors. This is Rylem's moment.
2. **Diversity certification is the moat:** FIS, Morningstar, Chewy, HubSpot, TELUS — all have active diversity supplier programs. Rylem's cert opens doors competitors can't walk through.
3. **Partnership model with larger agencies:** Solomon Page, Kforce, and other mid/large staffing firms NEED diverse subcontractors to meet their clients' diversity requirements. This is revenue without direct sales.
4. **Remote = nationwide:** Companies like GitLab, TELUS, Lumenalta, HubSpot hire remote — Rylem can place Seattle talent at NYC companies and vice versa.
5. **Mid-market sweet spot:** Companies with 500-5,000 employees are too big to recruit internally for everything but too small for the Randstads and Robert Halfs to prioritize. That's Rylem's lane.
6. **Vertical diversity is the pitch:** Most staffing agencies specialize in one vertical. Rylem covers six. For mid-market companies, one vendor across IT, finance, marketing, creative, HR, and admin is genuinely valuable.
