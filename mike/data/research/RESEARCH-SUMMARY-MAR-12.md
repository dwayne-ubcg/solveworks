## Research Methodology

### Sources Used
1. **Web search:** Built In Portland, Silicon Forest, Indeed, ZipRecruiter
2. **Web fetch:** Company homepages, careers pages (where available)
3. **Company research:** Business models, hiring signals, DEI initiatives, team size

### Filtering Criteria
- ✅ Mid-market size (300–3,000 employees) — not too large, not too small
- ✅ Active IT/tech hiring signals — job postings, recent news, growth trajectory
- ✅ Geographic: Pacific Northwest (WA, OR, CA)
- ✅ No apparent MSP exclusivity — mid-market companies use multiple vendors
- ✅ DEI-friendly culture — receptive to diversity-certified vendors

### Limitations
- Brave Search API rate limited (1 req/min on free plan) — switched to targeted fetches
- Some companies' careers pages blocked (Trupanion, Zillow) — used public research instead
- Limited to publicly available company data

## Risk Mitigation

### What Could Go Wrong?
1. **MSP lock-in:** Company already has exclusive staffing contract
   - *Mitigation:* Research their current vendors first, verify with quick call

2. **Wrong contact:** VP doesn't handle staffing decisions
   - *Mitigation:* Have secondary contacts ready (Head of HR, Talent Director)

3. **No response:** Common with cold emails
   - *Mitigation:* 3-email sequence + follow-up calls if warm intro available

4. **Budget constraints:** Company in hiring freeze
   - *Mitigation:* Adjust timing, circle back in 60 days
