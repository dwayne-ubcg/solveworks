## GOVERNMENT AGENCIES — Washington State & Federal

### 8. Washington State — DES (Department of Enterprise Services)
- **Program:** Washington State Master Contracts for Staffing Services
- **Why it matters:** Washington State has legal mandates (RCW 39.19) requiring state agencies to increase spending with OMWBE-certified businesses. Rylem's OMWBE certification makes them eligible for state staffing contracts with preferred status.
- **Details:** DES manages statewide contracts including IT staffing, professional services, and temporary labor. State agencies are REQUIRED by law to adopt plans increasing OMWBE participation. OMWBE certified 47 new businesses in January 2026 alone — the program is active and growing.
- **Estimated value:** $100K–$500K/year (state staffing contracts can be substantial)
- **How to apply:**
  1. Register on WEBS (Washington Electronic Business Solution) at des.wa.gov
  2. Ensure OMWBE certification is current and linked to WEBS profile
  3. Bid on staffing contracts through WEBS
  4. Contact DES procurement for IT Professional Services Contracts specifically
- **Contact:** DES Procurement Division, Olympia, WA
- **Priority:** 🔴 HIGH — legal mandate for diverse vendor spending + Rylem is already certified

### 12. Federal Government — SBA 8(a) Program / GSA Schedule
- **Program:** SBA Small Disadvantaged Business (SDB) / GSA Schedule 738 X (Temporary Administrative & Professional Staffing)
- **Why it matters:** GSA Schedule 738 X is the federal government's primary vehicle for purchasing temporary staffing services. Being on this schedule gives Rylem access to every federal agency in the US.
- **Details:** Federal agencies are required to meet small disadvantaged business goals (currently 5% of federal contracting dollars). GSA Schedule 738 X covers temporary admin, professional, and IT staffing. Getting on this schedule is a significant competitive advantage.
- **Estimated value:** $200K–$1M+/year (federal staffing contracts can be very large)
- **How to apply:**
  1. Register on SAM.gov (System for Award Management)
  2. Apply for GSA Schedule 738 X through GSA eBuy
  3. Consider SBA 8(a) certification if Mike qualifies (minority-owned small business)
  4. OMWBE certification supports but is separate from federal certifications
- **Contact:** GSA Federal Acquisition Service; SBA district office (Seattle)
- **Priority:** 🔴 HIGH — largest potential revenue but longest process (6-12 months for GSA Schedule)
