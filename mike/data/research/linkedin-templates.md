## HOW TO USE THESE

**Connection Requests** — 300 character limit. No pitch. Just a reason to connect.
**InMails** — Send after connecting OR cold via InMail credit. Keep under 150 words. One ask.
**Follow-Up** — Use 5–7 days after no reply. Different angle, same ask.

**Personalization tokens:**
- `[FIRST]` — their first name
- `[COMPANY]` — their company
- `[ROLE]` — their open role or job title
- `[CITY]` — their location
- `[SIGNAL]` — why you're reaching out (recent posting, news, growth, etc.)
