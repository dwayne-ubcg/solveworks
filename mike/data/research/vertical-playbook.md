## HOW TO USE THIS PLAYBOOK

- **Prospecting:** Match a company's open reqs to the relevant vertical. Use their job postings to build the cold email hook.
- **Pricing:** Bill rates below are ranges. Mid-market companies sit in the middle; enterprise skews high; startups/SMB skews low.

## VERTICAL 1: INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY (IT)

### Why IT Is Rylem's Anchor Vertical
IT staffing is the largest staffing market in the US (~$35B+). Contract/project-based IT work is the norm, churn is high, and companies constantly need specialized talent they can't find themselves. Rylem's diversity cert is a significant edge for government, defense, and enterprise IT contracts.

---

| Profile | Examples | Why They Buy Staffing |
|---|---|---|
| **Mid-market SaaS/tech companies (200–2,000 employees)** | Regional software companies, FinTech platforms, HealthTech | Growing fast, have budget, need specialized talent quickly — HR isn't equipped |
| **Large enterprise IT departments** | Banks, insurers, healthcare systems | Huge headcount, constant backfill, project-based surges (migrations, implementations) |
| **Federal/state government agencies + contractors** | DoD contractors, state DMVs, county agencies | Require diversity-cert vendors, multi-year contracts, less price-sensitive |
