Questions Answered

Do I need to build with SolveWorks after the workshop? ↓

No. The deliverables are yours. Take them to any vendor, build in-house, or put them in a drawer. The workshop has standalone value — you'll understand your operations better regardless of what you do next. That said, if you do want to build with us, the workshop investment applies toward setup.

Can the workshop be virtual? ↓

Yes. We run workshops both virtually (Zoom/Google Meet) and in-person. Virtual workshops are just as effective — we use shared digital whiteboards and collaborative documents in real-time.

Who should attend from my team? ↓

The business owner/decision-maker plus 1-2 people who know the day-to-day operations best. An office manager, operations lead, or senior team member who lives in the workflows we'll be mapping. Cap at 4-5 people for effectiveness.

What's the difference between $2,500 and $5,000? ↓

Complexity and scope. A single-location service business with straightforward workflows is on the lower end. Multi-location operations, complex tech stacks, or businesses needing deeper integration analysis are on the higher end. We'll scope it on our prep call and quote a fixed price before you commit.

Do I need any technical knowledge? ↓

Zero. We translate business operations into technical architecture. You bring the business knowledge; we bring the AI expertise. That's the whole point.

When do I receive the deliverables? ↓

Draft deliverables are created live during the session. Final polished versions — the process map, scorecard, architecture blueprint, and 90-day roadmap — are delivered within 48 hours of the workshop.
